Game Reserves

Kruger National Park

The Kruger Park is South Africa's largest wildlife sanctuary with nearly 2 million acres of wilderness and wildlife land. They are home to not only the Big Five, But also the widest range of mammals of any other African Game Reserves.

Location: Limpopo and Mpumalanga provinces

Accommodation:  Rest camps in the park offer a range of options from campsites to huts, bungalows, cottages safari tents and guest houses

Facilities: Includes restaurants, shops, petrol stations and more. YOu can either do a self-drive safari or get a guided game drive arranged

 

 

 

Addo Elephant Park

Addo is the third largest game reserve and is home to not only the Big Five, but the Big Seven which includes the Southern right whale and the Great White shark.
